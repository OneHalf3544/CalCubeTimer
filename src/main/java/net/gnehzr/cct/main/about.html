FAQ: What does CCT stand for?
Answer: C.A.L. Cube Timer.

Version 0.1 of CCT was developed by Jeremy Fleischman and Ryan Zheng during the ~5 months between the Berkeley Fall 2006 and Berkeley Spring 2007 Rubik's Cube Competitions. Version 0.2 was released June 2007, featuring many bug fixes and new features.
We hacked together version 0.9.0 over the summer of 2008, introducing many new features.
